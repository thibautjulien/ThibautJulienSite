About Me

Passionate about technology and development for the past 5 years, I am a graphic designer specialized in web design and currently undergoing training to become a web developer. At present, I focus solely on front-end development. By the end of my training, my expertise will cover the entire project lifecycle, from graphic design to production. I enjoy tackling challenges by creating innovative and effective solutions.

My goal ? To transform ideas into functional and aesthetically pleasing digital products. I believe in the importance of user experience, performance, and code quality. Every project is an opportunity for me to learn, grow, and contribute to a better digital world.

Outside of coding, I enjoy reading, staying updated with new technologies and their updates, outdoor activities, and spending time watching creative content, among other interests. All these activities help nourish my creativity and approach to projects.
